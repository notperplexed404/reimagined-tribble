Maldives Trip Plan (7–10 Days)

Crystal-clear lagoons, white-sand islands, water villas, and authentic island life — flexible for budget or luxury travelers.

Start: India Best time: Nov–Apr Visa-on-arrival Beaches & lagoons

Step 1: Travel to Maldives

  • Chennai / Mumbai → Malé (direct flights)
  • Bangalore / Kochi → Malé

Indian citizens get visa-on-arrival (30 days).

Expected round-trip: ₹20k–₹35k pp

Daily budget (per person)

  • Guesthouse: ₹3,000–₹6,000
  • Food: ₹1,500–₹2,500
  • Boat transfers: ₹1,500–₹4,000
  • Activities: ₹2,000–₹6,000

Pricing overview

Two styles: budget island stay OR resort luxury.

✅ 7-Day Maldives (Local Islands)

  • Flights: ₹20k–₹35k
  • Stay: ₹20k–₹35k
  • Food: ₹10k–₹15k
  • Activities: ₹8k–₹20k

Estimated: ₹60k–₹90k pp

✅ 7–10 Days (Resort Stay)

  • Flights: ₹20k–₹35k
  • Resort: ₹60k–₹1.5L+
  • Transfers: Speedboat / seaplane
  • Packages: Meals + activities

Estimated: ₹1L–₹2.2L pp
