Nepal Trip Plan (7–10 Days)

Affordable, complete, and scenic. Start from Chennai and cover all major highlights without missing the essentials.

Start: Chennai Best time: Oct–Apr Family-friendly Budget-focused

Step 1: Travel from Chennai to Nepal

  • Chennai → Delhi → Kathmandu (Air India/IndiGo)
  • Chennai → Kolkata → Kathmandu

Direct Chennai–Kathmandu flights are rare; a one-stop is normal and affordable.

Expected round-trip: ₹14k–₹22k pp (with pre-booking)

Budget to plan for (per day)

  • Hotels: ₹1,200–₹2,000 (clean budget stay)
  • Food: ₹400–₹700
  • Entry fees: ₹50–₹800 (temples/parks)
  • Transport: Local taxis, buses, tourist vans

Pricing overview

Transparent ranges for flights, stay, food, and activities — scale to 7 or 10 days.

✅ 7-Day Itinerary

  • Flights: ₹14k–₹22k (round-trip)
  • Hotels: ₹8,400–₹14,000 (₹1,200–₹2,000 × 7 nights)
  • Food: ₹2,800–₹4,900 (₹400–₹700 × 7 days)
  • Entry Fees: ₹350–₹5,600 (temples/parks)
  • Transport: Local taxis, buses, vans

Estimated: ~₹26k–₹47k pp

✅ 10-Day Itinerary

  • Flights: ₹14k–₹22k (same as 7-day)
  • Hotels: ₹12,000–₹20,000 (₹1,200–₹2,000 × 10 nights)
  • Food: ₹4,000–₹7,000 (₹400–₹700 × 10 days)
  • Entry Fees: ₹500–₹8,000 (temples/parks)
  • Transport: Local taxis, buses, vans

Estimated: ~₹30k–₹57k pp

7-Day Itinerary (short but covers all key places)

Day 1 — Arrive Kathmandu
Thamel check-in
+
  • Check-in at Thamel (best area for tourists)

Visit:

  • Garden of Dreams
  • Thamel Night Market (safe, touristy)

Try: Momo, sel roti, thukpa

Day 2 — Kathmandu Cultural Day
Temples & heritage
+
  • Pashupatinath Temple
  • Boudhanath Stupa
  • Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple)
  • Kathmandu Durbar Square
Affordable combo taxi day (₹1,200–₹1,800 for family)
Day 3 — Drive to Pokhara (6 hrs)
Scenic transfer
+

Budget option: Tourist AC bus (₹800–₹1,200)

Evening:

  • Lakeside walk
  • Boating at Phewa Lake
Day 4 — Pokhara Adventure Day
Sunrise & sights
+

Morning:

  • Sunrise at Sarangkot (super famous view)
  • Paragliding optional (₹4,000–₹5,000 — skip if too expensive)

Afternoon:

  • Davis Falls
  • Gupteshwor Cave
  • International Mountain Museum
Day 5 — Pokhara to Chitwan (4–5 hrs)
Jungle lodge
+

Check-in to a budget jungle lodge.

  • Evening cultural Tharu dance show
Day 6 — Chitwan Jungle Safari
Wildlife
+
  • Jeep Safari
  • Elephant Breeding Center
  • Canoe ride (safe guided)
  • Bird watching

Return to Kathmandu in the evening by bus.

Day 7 — Fly Back
Departure
+

Shopping at Thamel → Airport.

10-Day Itinerary (if you want everything)

Same as the 7-day plan, plus these add-ons:

Day 8 — Nagarkot Hill Station
Sunrise stay
+
  • Drive 1.5 hr from Kathmandu
  • Best sunrise spot in Nepal
  • Stay overnight to see the Himalayas from the balcony
Day 9 — Bhaktapur + Patan
Heritage
+
  • Bhaktapur Durbar Square
  • Pottery Square (you can try making pottery!)
  • Patan Museum
  • Golden Temple
Day 10 — Leisure + Fly Back
Wrap-up
+
  • Café hopping in Thamel
  • Souvenirs
  • Return flight to Chennai

What not to miss

Pashupatinath Temple
Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple)
Boudhanath Stupa
Pokhara + Sarangkot sunrise
Fewa Lake boating
Chitwan Jungle Safari
Bhaktapur + Patan
Momos, Thakali Thali
Optional: Paragliding / Nagarkot sunrise

Approx total cost per person (7–10 days)

  • Flights: ₹14k–₹22k
  • Hotels: ₹8k–₹15k (7–10 nights)
  • Food: ₹3k–₹6k
  • Internal travel: ₹4k–₹6k
  • Entry fees + activities: ₹2k–₹5k
  • Total: ≈ ₹30k–₹45k pp (family sharing = cheaper)
