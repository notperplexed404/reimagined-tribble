Malaysia Trip Plan (7–10 Days)

A perfect mix of modern cities, tropical islands, rainforests, and rich culture — affordable and beginner-friendly.

Start: India Best time: Nov–Apr Visa-free (India) Foodie paradise

Step 1: Travel to Malaysia

  • Chennai → Kuala Lumpur (direct / 1-stop)
  • Bangalore / Mumbai → Kuala Lumpur

Malaysia is visa-free for Indians (short stays) — very convenient.
