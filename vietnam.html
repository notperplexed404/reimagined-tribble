Vietnam Trip Plan (7–9 Days)

Street food, history, natural scenery, friendly locals, and one of the safest & cheapest international trips from India.

From India E-Visa Very Budget-Friendly ₹1L Goal

Flights & Entry

  • India → Hanoi / Ho Chi Minh City
  • Flight: ₹18k–₹30k
  • Vietnam E-Visa (easy)

Daily Budget

  • Hotels: ₹1.5k–₹3k
  • Food: ₹800–₹1.2k
  • Transport: ₹800–₹1.5k

7–9 Day Itinerary

Classic safe route: Hanoi → Ha Long Bay → Hoi An → Ho Chi Minh City

Day 1 — Hanoi
Arrival
+
  • Check-in + rest
  • Old Quarter walk
  • Try pho & banh mi
Day 2 — Hanoi
History
+
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum
  • Train Street (safe spots)
  • Evening food tour
Day 3 — Ha Long Bay
Nature
+
  • Day cruise or overnight boat
  • Kayaking & caves
  • Seafood dinner
Day 4 — Fly to Da Nang → Hoi An
Travel
+
  • Short domestic flight
  • Hoi An Ancient Town
  • Lantern streets at night
Day 5 — Hoi An
Relax
+
  • An Bang Beach
  • Cycling through rice fields
  • Tailor shops (optional)
Day 6 — Ho Chi Minh City
City
+
  • War Remnants Museum
  • Notre Dame Cathedral
  • Ben Thanh Market
Day 7 — Cu Chi Tunnels / Return
Optional
+

Half-day tour or rest → Fly back to India

Don’t Miss

Vietnamese street food
Ha Long Bay cruise
Lantern towns
History museums
Cheap transport
Safe solo travel

Total Estimated Cost

  • Flights: ₹18k–₹30k
  • Stay + food: ₹30k–₹40k
  • Transport + activities: ₹12k–₹18k
  • Total: ₹60k–₹85k per person
